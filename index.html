How this demo store works

This is a front-end demo built with React. It shows how products, categories and a cart could work on your site. In a real project this interface would connect to:

  • Your product catalogue or inventory system.
  • A secure payment gateway such as PayFast, PayStack or Stripe.
  • A simple admin area where you can manage products and orders.

As a client, you see exactly what your customers will experience before we connect it to your live products and payment provider.

About this portfolio demo

This page is designed as a portfolio example for an e-commerce project. It demonstrates:

  • Products placed directly under a strong heading for quick scanning.
  • Responsive grid for products and a sticky cart.
  • Interactive cart with quantities, totals and a mock checkout.

You can customise the colours, logo, content and products to match any brand – from a boutique store to a tech shop, clothing label or beauty brand.
